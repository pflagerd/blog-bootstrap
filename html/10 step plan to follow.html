10-Step Zero-Budget CPAP Battery Marketing Plan (DIY Everything)
Step 1: Foundation Setup (Week 1-2) - Budget: $0
Actions:
  • Brand name and domain: Use Wix.com or WordPress.com free tier (includes subdomain like yourname.wix.com)
  • Create brand assets:
    • Use Canva Free for logo design
    • GIMP (free Photoshop alternative) for advanced graphics
    • Use Google Fonts for consistent typography
  • Set up analytics: Google Analytics (free) and Google Search Console (free)
  • Create all social accounts: YouTube, Instagram, Facebook, TikTok, LinkedIn (all free)
  • Write brand guidelines: Use Google Docs (free) to document everything
DIY Tips:
  • Study competitor logos and create similar style in Canva
  • Use color palette generators online (coolors.co)
  • Screenshot and save all brand assets in Google Drive for consistency
Step 2: Website Development (Week 2-4) - Budget: $0
Actions:
  • Build website on Wix.com free plan: Choose health/medical template
  • Create essential pages: Home, Product, FAQ, Contact, Blog
  • Write all copy yourself: Use the messaging framework from your strategy doc
  • Optimize for mobile: Test on multiple devices using browser developer tools
  • Set up email capture: Use Wix's built-in forms + your Gmail account for responses
DIY Tips:
  • Study successful CPAP/medical device websites and model their structure
  • Use free stock photos from Unsplash, Pexels, Pixabay
  • Write compelling copy by addressing pain points directly
  • Create simple contact forms that send to your personal email
Step 3: Content Creation Setup (Week 3-4) - Budget: $0
Actions:
  • Use your smartphone: Most phones from last 5 years have excellent cameras
  • DIY lighting: Film near large windows during golden hour (1 hour before sunset)
  • Audio improvement:
    • Film in quiet rooms with soft furnishings (reduces echo)
    • Use your phone's voice memo app to test audio quality
  • Editing software: Download DaVinci Resolve (completely free, professional-grade)
  • Create content calendar: Use Google Sheets template
DIY Tips:
  • Stack books to create phone tripod
  • Use white sheets or walls as clean backgrounds
  • Record multiple takes - editing is easier than re-filming
  • Watch YouTube tutorials on smartphone filmmaking and DaVinci Resolve
Step 4: YouTube Channel Launch (Week 4-5) - Budget: $0
Actions:
  • Channel optimization:
    • Create banner in Canva Free using your brand colors
    • Write compelling channel description with keywords
    • Create playlists for different video series
  • Film first 3 videos:
    • Use natural lighting by windows
    • Create simple slideshow presentations in Google Slides for educational content
    • Film yourself explaining concepts clearly
  • Thumbnails: Design in Canva Free using bright colors and clear text
DIY Tips:
  • Study top CPAP/sleep channels and model their thumbnail styles
  • Use your phone's timer function for hands-free recording
  • Create simple graphics by screenshotting Google Slides presentations
  • Write detailed video descriptions with relevant keywords
Step 5: Social Media Content (Week 5-6) - Budget: $0
Actions:
  • Content creation:
    • Screenshot key moments from YouTube videos for Instagram posts
    • Use Canva Free to create quote graphics and educational carousels
    • Film short clips on your phone for TikTok/Reels
  • Scheduling: Use Creator Studio (Facebook/Instagram - free) and plan posts in advance
  • Hashtag research: Use free tools like Hashtagify or simply search competitor posts
DIY Tips:
  • Repurpose one YouTube video into 5-10 social media posts
  • Use your phone's built-in video editor for quick clips
  • Engage genuinely in sleep apnea Facebook groups (don't spam, just help people)
  • Share valuable tips and insights, not just product promotions
Step 6: Community Building (Week 6-7) - Budget: $0
Actions:
  • Join relevant communities:
    • Facebook groups for CPAP users, sleep apnea support
    • Reddit communities: r/SleepApnea, r/CPAP
    • Online forums and discussion boards
  • Provide value first: Answer questions, share tips, offer support
  • Build email list: Create valuable free guide (PDF) using Google Docs, offer in exchange for emails
  • Network with micro-influencers: Comment meaningfully on their posts, build relationships
DIY Tips:
  • Spend 30 minutes daily genuinely helping people in communities
  • Create a "CPAP Travel Guide" PDF as your lead magnet
  • Screenshot and save successful community posts for content inspiration
  • Never sell directly in groups - always provide value first
Step 7: Pre-Launch Buzz (Week 7-8) - Budget: $0
Actions:
  • Create anticipation content:
    • "Coming soon" posts showing product development
    • Behind-the-scenes videos of testing/quality checks
    • Educational content building up to the solution
  • Collect emails: Add signup forms to all your content
  • Reach out for partnerships:
    • Contact local sleep clinics via email
    • Message CPAP users who post in groups (offer free product for review)
DIY Tips:
  • Use your phone to film authentic "development journey" videos
  • Create countdown graphics in Canva
  • Write personalized emails to potential partners (not mass emails)
  • Offer genuine value in every interaction
Step 8: Launch Execution (Week 8) - Budget: $0
Actions:
  • Launch video: Create your "anchor" video explaining the problem and solution
  • Social media blitz: Post across all platforms simultaneously
  • Email announcement: Send to your collected list
  • Organic giveaway: "Share this post and tag 3 friends" for chance to win
  • Community engagement: Share launch news (appropriately) in relevant groups
DIY Tips:
  • Plan your launch day hour-by-hour
  • Respond to every single comment and message personally
  • Use free giveaway tools like Gleam.io's free tier
  • Cross-post content but adapt for each platform's style
Step 9: Consistent Content Creation (Week 9-20) - Budget: $0
Actions:
  • YouTube schedule: 1 video every 2 weeks minimum
  • Social media: Daily posts (mix of educational, user-generated, product)
  • Blog content: Write SEO articles using free keyword research (Ubersuggest free tier)
  • User testimonials: Actively request and film customer stories
  • Email newsletter: Weekly updates using your Gmail account
DIY Tips:
  • Batch create content - film 4 videos in one afternoon
  • Use Google Trends to find trending topics in your niche
  • Create templates for different types of content to speed up creation
  • Always respond to comments within 24 hours
Step 10: Analysis & Optimization (Week 10-ongoing) - Budget: $0
Actions:
  • Track everything:
    • YouTube Studio analytics
    • Google Analytics for website
    • Native social media insights
    • Email open rates
  • Weekly analysis: Spend 1 hour every Sunday reviewing what worked
  • Double down on success: Create more content similar to your best performers
  • A/B test: Try different thumbnail styles, posting times, content types
DIY Tips:
  • Keep a simple spreadsheet tracking all metrics
  • Screenshot your best-performing content to study patterns
  • Ask your audience directly what they want to see more of
  • Focus on engagement rate over follower count
Zero-Budget Tools Arsenal:
Design & Content Creation:
  • Canva Free: Graphics, thumbnails, social media posts
  • GIMP: Advanced photo editing
  • DaVinci Resolve: Professional video editing
  • Google Slides: Presentations and simple graphics
Website & Email:
  • Wix Free: Website builder
  • Google Analytics: Website tracking
  • Gmail: Email management and newsletter sending (small lists)
  • Google Forms: Contact forms and surveys
Social Media:
  • Creator Studio: Facebook/Instagram scheduling
  • TikTok Creator Tools: Built-in analytics
  • YouTube Studio: Video analytics and optimization
Research & Planning:
  • Google Trends: Keyword research
  • Answer The Public: Free content ideas
  • Ubersuggest Free: Basic SEO research
  • Google Sheets: Content calendars and tracking
Success Strategy for Zero Budget:
Time Investment:
  • Daily: 2-3 hours (content creation, community engagement, responding to comments)
  • Weekly: 1 day dedicated to batch content creation
  • Monthly: Half day analyzing results and planning next month
Key Success Factors:
  1. Consistency over perfection: Regular posting beats perfect content
  2. Authentic engagement: Real conversations build trust
  3. Value first: Always help before selling
  4. Long-term thinking: Building an audience takes 6-12 months
  5. Documentation: Track everything to learn what works
Expected Timeline for Results:
  • Month 1: Building foundation, learning curve
  • Month 2: Consistent content creation, slow growth
  • Month 3: Community building, first sales inquiries
  • Month 4-6: Steady growth, word-of-mouth marketing
  • Month 6+: Established audience, regular sales
Warning Signs to Watch:
  • Spending too much time on low-impact activities
  • Focusing on vanity metrics instead of engagement
  • Trying to be everywhere instead of mastering one platform first
  • Selling too hard too early
  • Inconsistent posting schedule
This zero-budget approach requires significant time investment but can be incredibly effective if executed consistently with genuine value-first mentality.
